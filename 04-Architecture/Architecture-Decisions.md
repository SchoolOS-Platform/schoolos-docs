🚨 Chief Architect's Decision

We're going to change our approach slightly.

Originally I planned to write the Problem Statement next.

I've changed my mind.

A professional consulting company would write the Business Case first because it answers the most important question:

Why should anyone invest time, money, or trust in SchoolOS?

Only after establishing the business value do we define the detailed problems and solution scope.

So our revised Discovery sequence becomes:

✅ Vision & Mission
✅ Stakeholder Register

🎯 Why the Business Case Matters

The Business Case isn't just another document.

It will be used when:

You present SchoolOS to Birdswood Secondary School.
You approach other schools.
You apply for funding.
You seek technology partners.
You present to the Department of Basic Education.
You speak with potential investors.

This document explains why SchoolOS exists, what value it creates, and why it deserves support.
