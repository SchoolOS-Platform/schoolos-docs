1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to identify every individual, group, or organization that has an interest in the SchoolOS platform. Understanding stakeholder expectations ensures that product decisions, technical architecture, and future enhancements are aligned with the needs of those who use, manage, or support the platform.

2. Introduction

SchoolOS is intended to serve a broad educational community. Although the pilot implementation will begin at Birdswood Secondary School, the platform is designed to scale to multiple educational institutions. Each stakeholder group has unique responsibilities, permissions, and expectations.

This register establishes those relationships and provides the foundation for role-based access control, user journeys, communication planning, and governance.

3. Stakeholder Categories

The stakeholders of SchoolOS are grouped into four categories:

Primary Stakeholders

Users who interact with the platform daily.

Learners
Teachers
Parents
School Administrators
Secondary Stakeholders

Individuals who support or oversee platform usage.

Principal
Deputy Principal
Heads of Department (HODs)
Administrative Staff
IT Support Personnel
Project Stakeholders

Individuals responsible for delivering and maintaining the platform.

Product Owner
Chief Software Architect
Future Development Team
UX/UI Designers
Quality Assurance Engineers
External Stakeholders

Organizations or individuals outside the school.

Department of Basic Education
Technology Partners
Community Partners
Future Investors
Educational Researchers
