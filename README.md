# SchoolOS

> Building the future of digital education.

SchoolOS is a modern education platform designed to simplify communication, teaching, learning and school administration through secure, scalable and user-centred technology.

---

## Vision

To create an education platform that empowers schools, educators, learners and parents through modern digital solutions.
